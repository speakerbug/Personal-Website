Rate My Best Feature

Rate My Best Feature was a project that was created out of the want to see how people interact with each other on a more social platform. The concept is simple; upload photos and people tell you what they think the best feature on you is in those photos. After uploading many photos and getting many results from strangers, the website will then be able to accurately determine what your overall best feature is. A Google Chrome extension was also created which allows you the opportunity to use the site while just surfing the web.

  • Client: Rate My Best Feature
  • Date: 2011
  • Service: Web Development • Google Chrome Development • Web Design • Database Management

Little URL

Little URL was built off of an open source platform but served as an opportunity to learn about real life coding, and finding a solution to a common problem; dealing with very long URL links. An iPhone app, Google Chrome extension, and several bookmarklets were all created as a result of Little URL. At it's peak, Little URL had four million active users, but due to lack of funding it unfortunately had to be closed in 2013.

  • Client: Little URL
  • Date: 2010
  • Service: Web Development • iPhone Development • Google Chrome Development • Database Management
